About Him

Born and raised within the charming but chaothic city of Buenos Aires. Now based in Barcelona to expand his domains.

His Music

Organic sounds with modern synthesizers, creating an atmosphere full of deep and melodic vibes enveloping you in a unique inner journey. Finding great inspiration from DJs like Hernan Cattaneo, Tiesto, Satori and others, Bodaishin welcomes you to his world of joy and fullness.

His Philosophy

The name Bodaishin comes from the Buddhist philosophical idea that predominates in his thoughts, actions and feelings. It means awakening of spiritual consciousness.
